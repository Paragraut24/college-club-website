Cultural Clubs


ViCulP

ViCulP is the official drama club of VIT. It actively engages in drama competitions like Firodia Karandak, Purushottam Karandak etc. year-round. We encourage participation in all facets of theater, encompassing acting, directing, and backstage roles. Embracing inclusivity and teamwork, we nurture talent and passion, fostering a dynamic environment where every student can shine on and off stage.


Zephyr

Join Zephyr, VIT's official dance club! We nurture dance talent and passion through workshops, rehearsals, and performances. From college events to inter-college competitions, we embody excellence and unity through dance. Be part of the rhythm!


Team Eklavya

Eklavya - The official streetplay team of VIT, conducts many street play acts on various different topics to spread awareness amongst the audience. Even in this difficult situation of pandemic, where the world has gone digital, we haven't stopped. We're trying our best to continue our activities in online mode by creating videos to educate and encourage people.


Abhivriddhi - Student Training and Development Cell

Abhivriddhi - Student Training and Development Cell is the Official Student Training and Development Committee of VIT, Pune. It conducts events to empower young minds with a diverse tapestry of skills, equipping them to conquer the corporate world and embrace the future with unshakable confidence. This mission is a symphony we conduct through meticulously crafted events and workshops, cultivating a culture of growth, development, and sheer excellence.


Vishwakarma Expression Hub

The Vishwakarma Expression Hub (VEH) is a vibrant community based in Pune, Maharashtra, dedicated to embracing the magic of art in various forms. Established in 2019, VEH brings together artists and writers who express themselves through writing articles and poetry, creating videos, capturing photos, graphic designing, and handmade paintings1. The club fosters a unique environment where all forms of art are celebrated under one umbrella, allowing members to freely express their creativity. With a team of passionate individuals, VEH not only promotes artistic expression but also nurtures a sense of family and community among its members.
