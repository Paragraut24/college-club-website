The Saarthi Club, at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is a dedicated organization aimed at empowering first-year students by addressing both academic and non-academic challenges they face. Founded in 2024, Saarthi operates as a non-profit entity, focusing on providing support and guidance to freshers, helping them navigate their initial year at VIT Pune. The club's initiatives are designed to foster a supportive community, ensuring that new students have the resources and assistance they need to succeed and thrive in their academic journey

The Saarthi Club : The mission of the Saarthi Club is to create a nurturing environment where first-year students can find the support they need to overcome the challenges of transitioning to college life. The club offers a range of services, including academic tutoring, mentorship programs, and workshops on various skills such as time management and stress relief. By addressing both academic and personal development, Saarthi aims to build a strong foundation for students, helping them achieve their full potential during their time at VIT Pune

In addition to its core mission, the Saarthi Club organizes various events and activities to promote social interaction and community building among students. These events include orientation programs, cultural festivals, and team-building exercises that encourage students to connect with their peers and faculty members. The club also collaborates with other student organizations and departments within VIT Pune to provide a holistic support system for first-year students. Through these efforts, Saarthi strives to create a vibrant and inclusive campus culture where every student feels valued and supported.

Overall, the Saarthi Club plays a crucial role in enhancing the student experience at VIT Pune. By offering comprehensive support and fostering a sense of community, the club helps first-year students navigate the complexities of college life and sets them on a path to success. Whether it's through academic assistance, personal development workshops, or social events, Saarthi is committed to making the transition to college as smooth and enjoyable as possible for all new students
