The Catalyst Club, under the Department of Engineering and Social Humanities (DESH) at VIT, Pune, was founded by Honorable Mahajan Sir. Its mission is to foster a strong connection between incoming students and faculty members, providing guidance and support throughout their undergraduate journey. The club is dedicated to enhancing the academic and personal growth of its members, creating a vibrant and inclusive learning community. Through various initiatives and activities, the Catalyst Club aims to build a supportive environment that encourages learning, collaboration, and personal development.

The Catalyst Club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is dedicated to empowering first-year students by providing a supportive community and diverse opportunities for personal and academic development. Here are some key details about the club:

Mission:
To help first-year students thrive academically, socially, and personally by fostering a vibrant environment for exploring interests, developing essential skills,
and building meaningful connections.
Academic Support:
Offers tailored programs such as study groups, tutoring sessions, and workshops on time management and study skills
to enhance the learning experience.
Skill Development:
Provides workshops and seminars on communication skills, leadership development, financial literacy,
and career readiness to equip students with essential life skills.
Personal Growth:
Focuses on holistic development through activities, retreats, and community service initiatives that promote
self-discovery, personal reflection, and character building.
Peer Mentorship:
Pairs first-year students with experienced upperclassmen who offer guidance, advice, and encouragement.
Networking and Community:
Organizes networking events, social gatherings, and campus-wide activities to connect
students with peers, faculty members, and guest speakers.
