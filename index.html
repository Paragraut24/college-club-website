FY Clubs

The Catalyst Vit

The Catalyst Club, under the Department of Engineering and Social Humanities (DESH) at VIT, Pune, was founded by Honorable Mahajan Sir.
Its mission is to foster a strong connection between incoming students and faculty members, providing guidance and support throughout their undergraduate journey.
The club is dedicated to enhancing the academic and personal growth of its members, creating a vibrant and inclusive learning community. Through various initiatives and activities, the Catalyst Club aims to build a supportive environment that encourages learning, collaboration,
and personal development.

The Saarthi Club

Saarthi is a club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, dedicated to empowering first-year students
by addressing both academic and non-academic challenges they face. Founded in 2024, Saarthi operates as a non-profit organization and focuses on providing support
and guidance to freshers, helping them navigate their initial year at VIT Pune. The club's initiatives are designed to foster a supportive community, ensuring
that new students have the resources and assistance they need to succeed and thrive in their academic journey

Tech Clubs

GedIT Coding Club

The GedIT Coding Club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is the official coding club of the institution,
dedicated to fostering a community of passionate coders and developers. Established in 2017, the club focuses on peer-to-peer learning
and skill development in various programming languages and technologies. It organizes events like competitive coding competitions, hackathons, and workshops,
providing students with opportunities to enhance their coding skills and collaborate on innovative projects. The club's activities are supported
by the Department of Information Technology and aim to create a vibrant coding culture within the campus

more about this club

Quark

Team Quark at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is a dynamic technical club dedicated to educating its members
about emerging technologies and fostering a passion for innovation. The club emphasizes the importance of collaboration among individuals with diverse perspectives to
generate creative ideas. Members are encouraged to explore various technologies with complete independence and flexibility, promoting a culture of continuous learning and
innovation. Team Quark organizes events like Hack://Envision, which includes expert sessions, hands-on projects, and national-level Capture The Flag (CTF)
competitions, providing students with valuable opportunities to enhance their skills in cybersecurity and other technical domains

Cultural Clubs

Zephyr

Zephyr is the official dance club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, known for its dynamic performances and unwavering
dedication. The club has earned a stellar reputation by participating in various competitions and consistently achieving remarkable results. Zephyr's
members are celebrated for their hardworking spirit and passion for dance, which have led them to secure top positions in prestigious events like the Mood
Indigo Desi Beats Finale and Flames University's cultural fest, Flames Kurukshetra. Their commitment to excellence and creativity makes Zephyr a standout
group within the VIT Pune community

ViCulP

ViCulP is the official drama club of VIT. It actively engages in drama competitions like Firodia Karandak,
Purushottam Karandak etc. year-round. We encourage participation in all facets of
theater, encompassing acting, directing, and backstage roles. Embracing inclusivity and teamwork, we nurture talent and passion, fostering a dynamic environment where every
student can shine on and off stage.
