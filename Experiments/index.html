FY Clubs

The Catalyst Club

The Catalyst Club, under the Department of Engineering and Social Humanities (DESH) at VIT, Pune, was founded by Honorable Mahajan Sir. Its mission is to foster a strong connection between incoming students and faculty members, providing guidance and support throughout their undergraduate journey.

The Saarthi Club

Saarthi is a club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, dedicated to empowering first-year students by addressing both academic and non-academic challenges they face. Founded in 2024, Saarthi operates as a non-profit organization and focuses on providing support and guidance to freshers.

Tech Clubs

GedIT Coding Club

The GedIT Coding Club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is the official coding club of the institution, dedicated to fostering a community of passionate coders and developers. Established in 2017, the club focuses on peer-to-peer learning and skill development in various programming languages and technologies.

Quark Team

Quark at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is a dynamic technical club dedicated to educating its members about emerging technologies and fostering a passion for innovation. The club emphasizes the importance of collaboration among individuals with diverse perspectives to generate creative ideas.

Cultural Clubs

Zephyr

Zephyr is the official dance club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, known for its dynamic performances and unwavering dedication. The club has earned a stellar reputation by participating in various competitions and consistently achieving remarkable results.

ViCulP

ViCulP is the official drama club of VIT. It actively engages in drama competitions like Firodia Karandak, Purushottam Karandak etc. year-round. We encourage participation in all facets of theater, encompassing acting, directing, and backstage roles.
